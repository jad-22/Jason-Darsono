About Me

For about three years, I served as a Regional Operations Analyst at Shopee, the largest e-commerce force in SEA. Throughout my tenure, I played a significant role in driving enhancements across both marketplace and logistics functions within the company. My experience was enriched by extensive collaboration with a wide spectrum of stakeholders, from technical to non-technical including Business Development, Product Managers, and Data Scientists.

Combining my professional experience with my academic journey through the Business Analytics master's programme at Imperial College London, I have built proficiency in data manipulation, visualization, and analysis using SQL, Python, and R. My academic training encompasses statistical analysis, machine learning and data management, with practical application through projects undertaken during my studies. I am adept at crafting impactful data presentations using PowerPoint and also in utilising BI tools like Tableau and Power BI to create dashboards and communicate insights.

I am actively broadening my knowledge by reading during my leisure time, as well as dedicating time to side projects. I am also motivated to improve my writing skills by contributing on Medium in the near future. Feel free to reach out, and I would be thrilled to connect with you on LinkedIn!
