Project Highlights

Topic Modelling on User Web Activities

A user's web journey tells a lot about the user's preferences. By modelling the topic content of the websites visited, we deepen our understanding of the users' interests and potentially perform further user segmentation and/or recommendation.

  • More

Marketing Mix Modelling on Promotion Effectiveness

To increase sales, retail stores rely on in-store promotions and pricing strategies. But how effective are the promotions? How will consecutive and concurrent promotions affect sales? Using the SCAN*PRO model, we assess the static, temporal and cannibalisation effects of retail marketing strategies.

  • More

Daily Energy Demand Forecasting

In the energy market, accurate forecasts are necessary in order to optimise the schedule and balance between load demand and generation. By using historical energy demand, weather forecasts and other features, this project aims to build a model capable of accurately forecasting future 2-day demand.

Heat Smart Orkney Business Strategy

Orkey is a major exporter of wind turbine energy in the UK and curtailment is a downside faced by wind farmers there. Here, we use smart meter data to model wind energy generation, estimate curtailment and provide business recommedation to penetrate the smart energy market in Orkney.
